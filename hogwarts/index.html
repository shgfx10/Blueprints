06 // KEY LOCATIONS

🏰 Great Hall

Central dining hall with enchanted ceiling. Houses 4 house tables and staff table.

📚 Library

Over 10,000 books. Restricted Section requires teacher permission.

🔮 Divination Classroom

Located in North Tower. Professor Trelawney's domain.

🧪 Potions Dungeon

Underground chambers. Cold, damp, and full of mysterious ingredients.

🚪 Chamber of Secrets

Hidden chamber beneath school. Contains Basilisk. Founded by Salazar Slytherin.

⭐ Room of Requirement

Appears only when needed. Contains anything the requester requires.

🐈 Headmaster's Office

Contains Sorting Hat, Pensieve, and Fawkes the Phoenix.

🏓 Quidditch Pitch

30 meter tall goal posts. Field length: 150m x 60m.

07 // MAGICAL PROTECTION SYSTEMS

🔮 Fidelius Charm

Hides the castle's location within a secret keeper. Cannot be found unless the secret is revealed.

Power Level EXTREME

🛡️ Anti-Muggle Charms

Muggles see only ruins and a sign warning them away. Cannot perceive the castle.

Radius ~5 MILES

⚡ Unplottable

Castle cannot be plotted on any map. Does not exist in non-magical cartography.

Effect ABSOLUTE

🏰 Ward Shields

Multiple defensive barriers. Repels dark magic and hostile creatures.

Strength LEGENDARY
