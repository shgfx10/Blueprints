01 // OVERVIEW

Designation

The RX-78 Gundam is a prototype mobile suit developed by the Earth Federation as part of Operation V. It was the first mobile suit to use Luna Titanium armor.

Status LEGENDARY

Designer

Developed at Side 7 by Tem Ray. Features revolutionary movable frame technology and twin eye cameras for enhanced targeting.

Manufacturer ANAHEIM ELECTRONICS

Pilot

Amuro Ray, a 15-year-old civilian, became its pilot during the One Year War. The Gundam became a symbol of hope for the Federation.

Fame WHITE DEATH

05 // WEAPON SYSTEMS

PRIMARY

Beam Rifle (XHB-79)

Standard energy weapon with 0.9 MW output. Powered by E-cap system. Effective range: 3,400 meters.

SHIELD

Luna Titanium Shield

1.8m tall defensive shield. Can withstand 3 direct hits from a Zaku machine gun. Doubles as melee weapon.

MELEE

Beam Saber (SB-13)

High-energy plasma blade stored in backpack. Cuts through mobile suit armor. 2 units carried.

VESTIGAL

Vulcan Pods (60mm)

Head-mounted autocannons. 120 rounds total. Effective against missiles and lightly armored targets.

SPECIAL

Hyper Bazooka (H&B S-G2)

Optional weapon. 280mm anti-ship bazooka. High explosive rounds. Used against Musai-class ships.

OPTIONAL

Giant Bazooka (Type B)

Heavy ordnance for ship-to-ship combat. Carries 4 rockets per reload. Used in final battle.
